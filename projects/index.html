CaTS-Bench

Built a benchmark for context-aware time series captioning across 11 domains. Developed a data pipeline for plot-image, metadata, and caption triplets, and produced 1700 human-rewritten gold captions with strict numeric and temporal verification.

Multimodal Alignment Across Time Series, Vision, and Language

Implemented trimodal contrastive training with frozen text, vision, and time-series encoders plus trainable projection heads. Evaluated alignment using InfoNCE objectives and geometric metrics including Procrustes distance, CKA, mutual kNN, and retrieval at k.

Motion Tape ML

Developed a machine learning stack for low back pain classification using 6-channel strain data sampled at 60 Hz across 19 movements. Engineered global and unloading-dynamics features and trained logistic regression and random forest soft-voting models under 100-fold leave-pair-out cross-validation.

Evaluation Suite for Vision-Language and Language Models

Implemented batched inference runners, prompt templates, and evaluation metrics for more than 18 VLMs and LLMs. Combined standard language metrics with custom numeric fidelity and temporal matching diagnostics.

Marathi Sentiment and Emotion Analysis

Implemented a hybrid model combining horse herd optimization, bidirectional RNNs, and cognitive computing for Marathi sentiment and emotion analysis, achieving F1 scores of 0.967 and 0.978.

Chronic Kidney Disease Prediction

Worked on metaheuristic feature selection and classification methods for chronic kidney disease prediction, improving accuracy by 19 percent over traditional feature extraction approaches.
