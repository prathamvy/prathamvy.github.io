Hi, I’m Pratham Yashwante, a Master’s student in Computer Science at the University of California, San Diego. I work in the Spatiotemporal ML Lab and am advised by Prof. Rose Yu.

Research

My research interests include multimodal machine learning, time series understanding, representation learning, and vision-language models. I am especially interested in how models can reason more faithfully across text, vision, and numeric time series, and in building evaluation frameworks that expose failures in temporal grounding, scientific reasoning, and multimodal alignment.

Recently, I have worked on time series captioning benchmarks, contrastive alignment across time series, vision, and language, and machine learning for health and biomechanical sensor data.
