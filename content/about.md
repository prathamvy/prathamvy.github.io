I am a Master's student in Computer Science at **UC San Diego**, where I work in the **Spatiotemporal ML Lab** advised by **Prof. Rose Yu**.

My research lies at the intersection of **multimodal machine learning**, **time series understanding**, and **representation learning**. I am especially interested in how models can reason more faithfully across **text, vision, and numeric time series**, and in building evaluation frameworks that reveal failures in grounding, temporal understanding, and scientific reasoning.

My recent work includes:
- **time series supervision** for physical reasoning in VLMs
- **multimodal contrastive alignment** across text, image, and time-series encoders
- **CaTS-Bench**, a benchmark for numeric time series captioning
- **Motion Tape ML**, a low back pain classification project using multi-sensor strain data
- evaluation pipelines for large vision-language and language models across grounded reasoning tasks

Before UC San Diego, I completed my **Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering** from **SIES Graduate School of Technology, University of Mumbai**, graduating with a GPA of **9.47/10**. I received the **Technical Excellence Award 2024** and the **Outstanding Technical Achievement Award 2023**.
